Jim Carrey is a comedian and actor that is well known for his 90s comedy movies. Carrey was born in Canada on January 17, 1962 and was raised in poverty with a fincally unstable family, he had to work two jobs for 8 hours a day to help his family. He first gained recongition in 1990 for his star role in the show Living Color. Some of his popular movies were Ace Ventura: Pet Detective, followed up with Liar Liar, Dumb and Dumber, and Ace Ventura: When Nature Calls. At only 15 years old, he preformed comedy at a Toronto comedy club and gained lots of attention for himself. Carrey moved to Hollywood when he was 19 to pursue his comedian career. His first on show comedy act was in 1983 is the film Introducing...Janet. He is continuing to act with films such as Sonic and the Hedgehog (2020) and appearing in a few episodes on Saturday Night Live.

I chose Jim Carrey as my hero or the person that I look up to because he started off with almost nothing, but he never gave up and worked hard until he got to a point where he was on top and helped his family finacially. I admire that dedication going from nothing to one of the most popular actors of all time. His journey has inspired me to persevere more and work hard to achieve things; Carrey's movies are also really funny and reminds me of my comedy. I have watched his movies a lot with my dad and I, his way of acting is why he is my favorite actor and why he has impacted me.
